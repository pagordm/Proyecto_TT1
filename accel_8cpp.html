Detailed Description

Implementation of satellite acceleration computations.

This file contains functions to compute the acceleration of an Earth-orbiting satellite, including the effects of Earth's harmonic gravity field, gravitational perturbations from the Sun and Moon, solar radiation pressure, and atmospheric drag.
