Detailed Description

Declarations for satellite acceleration computations.

This header file contains the declaration for the Accel function, which computes the acceleration of an Earth-orbiting satellite, including the effects of Earth's harmonic gravity field, gravitational perturbations from the Sun and Moon, solar radiation pressure, and atmospheric drag.

Computes the acceleration of an Earth orbiting satellite.

Calculates acceleration due to:

  • Gravitational perturbations of Sun and Moon
